The median selection algorithm is a subset of selection algorithms. A selection algorithm is an algorithm for finding the kth smallest number in a list or array; such a number is called the kth order statistic.

The median is the value separating the higher half from the lower half of a data sample (a population or a probability distribution). For a data set, it may be thought of as the "middle" value. To simplify the problem we focus on the (floor(array.size/2) + 1)-smallest element. This means that we ignore the case when the list has an even amount of elements.
